Intelligence vs Luck — Measurement Framework
Catalyst Hit Rate
% of trades where price moved ≥ 0.5% in expected direction within 60 min. High rate = research is real edge, not lucky guesses.
Target: > 60%
Gate Respect Score
% of trades where all 9 entry gates were logged as passed. Below 100% = discipline problem, not strategy problem.
Target: 100%
Edge Stability
Rolling 5-trade win rate plotted over time. Flat = consistent edge. Decaying = overfitting. Rising = learning.
Target: flat or rising, ≥ 50%
R-Quality
Avg R on winners vs losers. If wins ≈ +0.5R and losses ≈ -1R, the system has positive EV structure regardless of luck.
Target: avg win > 0.5R
